Yes, absolutely! Before any stump grinding work begins, customers must confirm the location of all underground utilities including gas lines, fiber optic cables, electrical lines, water pipes, and sewer lines. This is a critical safety requirement.

  • ‚Ä¢ Call 811 (North Carolina 811) at least 2-3 business days before your scheduled service. We must receive the order details from 811 (pig tail Form) faxed to us before work can be started.
  • ‚Ä¢ We will Explain these requirements in more detail on our phone consultation. Please Mark all utility locations with flags or spray paint
